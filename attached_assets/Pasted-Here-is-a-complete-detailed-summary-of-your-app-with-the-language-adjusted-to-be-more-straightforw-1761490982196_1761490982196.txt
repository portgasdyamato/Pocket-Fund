Project Summary: "The Financial Glow-Up"
"The Financial Glow-Up" is a gamified financial web app designed for young adults in India. It transforms the often-stressful topics of financial literacy and saving into an active, engaging, and motivational experience called "The Fight."

The app's persona is a "Financial Partner"—it's designed to be an encouraging and non-judgmental guide. It helps you understand your spending habits, challenge your impulse buys (called "Icks" in the app), and celebrate every "Win," all while helping you build real, tangible savings.

The Core User "Loop"
The app is built on a simple, effective 4-step loop:

TRACK (Get Your Data): The user securely links their bank account one time using the Account Aggregator (AA) framework. This gives the app a read-only feed of all their transactions (including all UPI spending).

FIGHT (The Daily Task): The user's main daily task is to visit "The Fight" page and categorize their transactions as "Needs" (rent, bills), "Wants" (coffee, dining), or "Icks" (impulse buys, wasteful spending).

STASH (The "Win"): The app challenges the user to "fight back" against their "Icks." Using a pre-approved Razorpay UPI AutoPay Mandate, the user can "Stash" cash with a single tap. This instantly moves real money from their bank into their personal "Locker."

GROW (The Glow-Up): The "Locker" is a real Liquid Mutual Fund account in the user's name, where their stashed cash grows and earns interest. Every "Stash" builds their "Save Streak" and unlocks achievements in their "Trophy Case," creating a powerful loop of positive reinforcement.

App Structure & User Flow
1. Onboarding (The "Bootcamp")
A one-time, 4-step wizard for all new users after they sign up (via Google Auth):

Step 1: Set Quest: User names their first savings goal (e.g., "Europe Trip," "New Laptop").

Step 2: Connect Intel: User links their bank via Account Aggregator.

Step 3: Unlock Locker: User completes a 2-min eKYC to create their Liquid Mutual Fund account.

Step 4: Enable Stash: User approves the Razorpay UPI Mandate to enable one-tap payments.

2. The Logged-In App (Main Pages)
Once onboarded, the user navigates the app using a persistent navbar:

HQ (Dashboard): The main landing page. Shows a summary of their "Main Quest" progress, their current "Save Streak," and a prompt for their "Daily Fight" (e.g., "You have 8 new transactions to review!").

The Fight (Transactions): The daily action page. A feed of all bank transactions where the user tags their spending as "Needs," "Wants," or "Icks."

The Glow-Up (Wallet/Locker): The real-money "wallet." Users can see their total "Locker" balance (including interest), manage all their savings goals, "Stash" more cash, or "Cash Out" (withdraw) back to their bank.

Level Up (Quests & Learning): The financial literacy hub, gamified as "Quests." Users complete bite-sized lessons (e.g., "The Subscription Slayer," "Credit Score 101") to earn badges.

Trophy Case (Badges & Streaks): The motivation hub. A visual gallery of all unlocked badges ("First ₹1,000 Stashed," "10-Day Streak") and a calendar tracking their streaks.

Profile (Settings): Users manage their connected accounts, KYC status, notification preferences, and log out.
